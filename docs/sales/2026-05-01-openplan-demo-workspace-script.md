# OpenPlan Demo Workspace Script

_Date: 2026-05-01_
_Status: Internal guided-demo script; proof-indexed operator notes, not outbound copy._

## Demo Goal

Show OpenPlan as a civic planning workbench where projects, reports, grants, engagement, analysis, admin support, and billing posture share one evidence spine.

Do not demo it as a generic AI dashboard or a fully self-serve SaaS checkout product.

## Operator Rule For The Walkthrough

Before moving to the next section, name both items below out loud or in the screen-share notes:

1. **Underlying proof artifact / evidence link** — the specific smoke, proof packet, or service document supporting the step.
2. **Caveat boundary** — what the proof does not claim yet, especially around self-serve activation, modeling validity, billing, legal/compliance, AI autonomy, survey-grade/aerial claims, and buyer-specific acceptance.

## 30-Minute Flow

### 0. Setup - 2 minutes

Say:

> OpenPlan is Apache-2.0 open-source planning software with Nat Ford managed hosting, onboarding, implementation, support, and planning services. This demo focuses on supervised planning workflows that are already proof-backed.

Do:

- Confirm this is a guided proof-of-capability demo.
- Name the buyer's likely first workflow.

Proof artifact:

- [Release-to-sale plan](../ops/2026-05-01-openplan-release-to-sale-plan.md)
- [Buyer-safe caveat sheet](2026-05-01-openplan-buyer-safe-caveat-sheet.md)

Caveat boundary:

- This is a supervised planning workbench demo, not a fully self-serve municipal SaaS launch, autonomous AI planning claim, legal/compliance automation claim, or finished all-in-one suite claim.
- The first buyer workflow still needs scoped onboarding, reviewer ownership, data-sensitivity review, and buyer-specific acceptance checks.

### 1. Command Center / Workspace Posture - 4 minutes

Show:

- workspace shell,
- active planning surfaces,
- evidence-first navigation,
- support/billing boundaries if relevant.

Point:

- The workspace is the shared planning record, not a disconnected dashboard.

Proof artifact:

- [Phase 1 shared-spine smoke](../ops/2026-05-02-openplan-local-spine-smoke.md)
- [Admin pilot readiness proof packet](2026-05-01-openplan-admin-pilot-readiness-proof-packet.md)

Caveat boundary:

- The shared-spine proof is local/synthetic release evidence, not buyer-specific production acceptance.
- Do not imply that every future module or export is automatically covered without its own proof row and stale/readiness recalculation.

### 2. RTP / Report Packet - 6 minutes

Show:

- RTP cycle or report registry,
- packet/detail page,
- generated HTML artifact,
- release-review/stale-basis posture.

Proof artifact:

- [RTP release-review smoke](../ops/2026-05-01-openplan-local-rtp-release-review-smoke.md)

Caveat boundary:

- The proof shows workflow continuity from cycle to packet to artifact and release-review landing.
- It is not an adopted board packet, official agency record, legal sign-off, or promise that a buyer's production RTP can rely on demo assumptions without local review.

### 3. Grants / Funding / Reimbursement - 5 minutes

Show:

- opportunity/award,
- project funding posture,
- reimbursement invoice,
- closeout milestone.

Proof artifact:

- [Grants flow smoke](../ops/2026-05-01-openplan-local-grants-flow-smoke.md)

Caveat boundary:

- The proof shows project funding posture, award/reimbursement/closeout continuity, and RTP write-back.
- It is planning support only: no certified grant scoring, award likelihood guarantee, reimbursement compliance sign-off, or replacement for funding-source/professional review.

### 4. Engagement Handoff - 4 minutes

Show:

- public portal/share intake,
- moderation queue,
- approved public feedback,
- handoff report artifact.

Proof artifact:

- [Engagement report handoff smoke](../ops/2026-05-01-openplan-local-engagement-report-handoff-smoke.md)

Caveat boundary:

- The proof shows intake, moderation, publication, report provenance, HTML artifact generation, and source-context traceability.
- It does not replace an agency's official outreach plan, public-records retention duties, legal noticing, language-access commitments, or buyer-specific moderation policy.

### 5. Analysis / Model Run Linkage - 4 minutes

Show:

- managed run or analysis result,
- scenario attachment,
- Analysis Studio deep link,
- analysis-summary report.

Proof artifact:

- [Analysis report linkage smoke](../ops/2026-05-01-openplan-local-analysis-report-linkage-smoke.md)
- [Modeling caveat KPI SQL gate proof](../ops/2026-05-08-openplan-modeling-caveat-kpi-sql-gate-proof.md)

Caveat boundary:

- The proof shows analysis output linkage into scenario/report artifacts and the current behavioral-onramp caveat gate.
- Do not describe the demo as validated behavioral forecasting, calibrated production demand modeling, certified model validation, or autonomous model interpretation.

### 6. Admin / Support / Billing Posture - 3 minutes

Show or summarize:

- request-access intake,
- allowlisted reviewer triage,
- provisioned workspace and owner invite path,
- billing waiver language.

Proof artifact:

- [Local admin/support flow smoke](../ops/2026-05-01-openplan-local-admin-support-flow-smoke.md)
- [Production admin operations authenticated smoke](../ops/2026-05-01-openplan-production-admin-operations-authenticated-smoke.md)
- [Billing current-cycle waiver proof](../ops/2026-05-01-openplan-billing-current-cycle-waiver-proof.md)

Caveat boundary:

- Request access is supervised; it does not automatically provision workspaces or create outbound commitments.
- Billing posture is historical live-payment evidence plus current non-money-moving proof; no fresh same-cycle paid canary is claimed.
- RPO/RTO, support targets, SSO/private hosting, and enhanced SLA terms are filled per managed-hosting engagement, not promised globally.

### 7. Close - 2 minutes

Ask:

- Which first workflow should we scope?
- Who owns review?
- What data sensitivity or procurement constraint matters?
- Is this self-hosted, managed-hosted, implementation-only, or a mix?

Capture before the next step:

- the buyer's single highest-value workflow,
- the accountable reviewer or review group,
- any public-records, PII, procurement, or hosting constraint,
- and the service lane that should frame follow-up: self-hosted, managed hosting, implementation-only, planning support, or a blended lane.

Proof artifact:

- [Pilot SOW template](2026-05-01-openplan-pilot-sow-template.md)
- [Managed-hosting service schedule](2026-05-01-openplan-managed-hosting-service-schedule.md)
- [Buyer-safe caveat sheet](2026-05-01-openplan-buyer-safe-caveat-sheet.md)

Caveat boundary:

- The close should produce a scoped follow-up question or pilot frame, not an unbounded implementation promise.
- Do not commit pricing, support SLA, RPO/RTO, paid activation, procurement posture, official-record retention, or production modeling reliance until the relevant schedule/SOW fields and proof needs are reviewed.

## Do Not Say

- "The model is validated forecasting."
- "AI makes the planning decision."
- "Billing was freshly paid-canary-proven today."
- "Public request access automatically creates a workspace."
- "This replaces legal or professional review."
- "Aerial or field evidence is survey-grade or certified without the scoped evidence package and professional review."
