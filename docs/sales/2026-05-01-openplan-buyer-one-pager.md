# OpenPlan Buyer One-Pager

_Date: 2026-05-01_
_Status: Buyer-safe draft; review before external send._

## The Offer

OpenPlan is an Apache-2.0 planning operating system for transportation and community planning. Nat Ford helps agencies and consultants implement it, host it, customize it, and use it to deliver better planning work.

The near-term sale is not "AI writes your plan" and not broad self-serve municipal SaaS. The near-term sale is a supervised planning workbench where projects, plans, funding, maps, model runs, public comments, reports, and evidence stay connected.

## Who It Is For

- Rural RTPAs, counties, and small transportation commissions with limited staff capacity.
- Cities and towns managing plans, grants, project lists, and public-facing maps.
- Tribes needing LRTP, TTIP/TIP, safety, grant, and infrastructure evidence support.
- Consultants who manage multiple planning clients and need faster, more defensible deliverables.
- MPOs or state/regional agencies that need program coordination and proof-backed reporting.

## What OpenPlan Helps With Today

- Project and workspace records.
- RTP/report packet workflow proof.
- Grants, funding, reimbursement, and closeout posture.
- Engagement intake, moderation, and report handoff.
- Analysis/model run output linked into reports.
- Admin/support intake, reviewer triage, provisioning, and owner invitations.
- Managed-hosting billing posture with an explicit current-cycle paid-canary waiver.

## Why It Is Different

1. It understands planning workflows: RTPs, grants, public comments, project lists, funding cycles, model runs, and board packets are native concepts.
2. It connects evidence: project records, maps, datasets, comments, scenarios, reports, and exports share one spine.
3. It is open-source first: agencies can inspect the code and avoid a closed vendor dependency.
4. It is rural/tribal/small-agency friendly: it does not assume a large internal modeling or GIS shop.
5. It treats AI as supervised assistance: human review and evidence remain central.
6. It produces deliverables, not only dashboards.

## Commercial Lanes

- **Self-hosted core:** Apache-2.0 software; no OpenPlan license fee.
- **Managed hosting + support:** Nat Ford operates the hosted workspace and support path, starting from the public service baseline.
- **Implementation + planning services:** onboarding, data setup, workflow configuration, reports, integrations, training, and planning support scoped by engagement.

## Buyer-Safe Proof Boundary

Use this language:

> OpenPlan is ready for supervised pilots and managed-service engagements where the workflow is scoped, evidence-backed, and human-reviewed.

Do not claim:

- fully self-serve SaaS,
- validated behavioral forecasting,
- complete LAPM/legal compliance automation,
- autonomous AI planning,
- or a fresh same-cycle paid checkout canary.

## First Call Goal

Do not sell every future module. Pick one workflow to prove first:

1. RTP/report packet,
2. grants and reimbursement,
3. engagement-to-report handoff,
4. analysis/report linkage,
5. or managed-hosting setup.
