# OpenPlan Managed-Hosting Service Description

_Date: 2026-05-01_
_Status: Buyer-safe draft; review before external send. Use `2026-05-01-openplan-managed-hosting-service-schedule.md` when a buyer needs an attachable service schedule._

## Service Summary

Nat Ford can operate OpenPlan as a managed service for agencies and consultants that want the open-source planning system without running their own infrastructure.

Managed hosting is a service wrapper around the Apache-2.0 OpenPlan core. It is not a proprietary seat-license claim and not a promise of fully self-serve municipal SaaS.

## Included Baseline Duties

- Hosted OpenPlan workspace operation.
- Workspace activation and owner invitation path.
- Basic user/access coordination during onboarding.
- Service-lane intake through `/request-access`.
- Billing/support ledger for hosted workspace posture.
- Reasonable backups and operational monitoring appropriate to the selected service tier.
- Email support following the active managed-hosting service schedule; if no schedule is attached, Nat Ford targets acknowledgement within 2 business days.
- Guided onboarding session for the first selected workflow.

## Optional Managed-Service Add-Ons

- Additional workspaces or client workspaces.
- Data migration and cleanup.
- Report template setup.
- Public engagement portal setup.
- Grant/funding setup.
- Analysis/model setup and caveat review.
- Aerial/field evidence setup.
- Private cloud, SSO, or agency-cloud deployment scoping.
- Dedicated support/SLA.

## Boundaries

Managed hosting does not include, unless separately contracted:

- unlimited planning services,
- unlimited custom software work,
- legal or regulatory sign-off,
- calibrated forecasting validation,
- automated LAPM compliance completion,
- 24/7 incident response,
- or public claims that the workspace is ready for broad self-serve use without human onboarding.

## Support Path

1. Client submits or confirms service lane.
2. Nat Ford confirms workspace owner, first workflow, data sensitivity, and billing/support posture.
3. Nat Ford provisions or confirms the hosted workspace.
4. Owner invite is delivered manually unless email automation is separately configured.
5. First workflow is configured and reviewed with the client.
6. Support questions go through the named support contact and escalation path.

## Billing Proof Boundary

For the May 1, 2026 release-to-sale packet, billing is positioned as historical live payment evidence plus current non-money-moving billing proof. A fresh same-cycle paid checkout canary was waived and must not be described as freshly re-proven.
