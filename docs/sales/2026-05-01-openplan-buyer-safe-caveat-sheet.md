# OpenPlan Buyer-Safe Caveat Sheet

_Date: 2026-05-01_
_Status: External-safe caveat copy; attach to pilot/SOW packets when useful._

## Production-Backed Enough For Supervised Sale

- Authenticated workspaces and role-scoped access.
- Project, plan, program, scenario, model, report, and engagement surfaces.
- Shared project-spine reuse across RTP, grants, engagement, analysis, reports, maps, Data Hub, and aerial evidence.
- RTP/report packet creation and artifact generation.
- Grants/funding/reimbursement and closeout posture.
- Engagement intake, moderation, public publication, and report handoff.
- Managed analysis/model run output linked to scenarios and reports.
- Admin request-access, reviewer triage, provisioning, and owner invitation flow.
- Billing infrastructure and support posture, with explicit paid-canary waiver.

## Prototype / Beta / Scoped Carefully

- Advanced transportation modeling and scenario analysis.
- AequilibraE/ActivitySim/MATSim direction.
- Data Hub depth and external GIS integrations.
- Aerial/field evidence workflows beyond scoped implementation.
- AI-assisted drafting and summarization.
- Private cloud, SSO, and enterprise integrations.

## Not Yet Supported As A Broad Claim

- Fully self-serve municipal SaaS.
- Autonomous planning decisions.
- Validated behavioral forecasting.
- Complete LAPM/legal/compliance automation.
- Certified grant scoring or guaranteed funding competitiveness.
- 24/7 support unless separately contracted.
- Automatic workspace creation from public request-access.
- Fresh same-cycle paid checkout canary.

## Required Language

Use:

> OpenPlan supports supervised planning workflows with evidence traceability and human review.

Use:

> Modeling and AI outputs are planning-support materials and must be reviewed before external reliance.

Use:

> Managed hosting and implementation are service wrappers around the Apache-2.0 open-source core.

Avoid:

> OpenPlan replaces planners, consultants, engineers, attorneys, or compliance reviewers.

Avoid:

> OpenPlan is already a fully self-serve municipal SaaS platform.

Avoid:

> Billing was freshly re-proven with a paid checkout in this cycle.

## Buyer Qualification Questions

1. What is the first workflow you need to prove?
2. Who owns review and approval?
3. What data can be public, internal, or confidential?
4. Do you want self-hosting, managed hosting, implementation services, or a mix?
5. What deliverable must exist at the end of the first 30-90 days?
