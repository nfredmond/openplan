## Notes

- "Closed" means: a current proof doc demonstrates the original risk no longer fires under the gates we run. It does not mean the underlying surface area is frozen — regressions are caught by `pnpm qa:gate` + the May 1 RC proof gate.
- "Carried forward" means: the original risk has mitigation/proof links, but still constrains release language or implementation posture.
- "Open" means: the caveat is real, named in a proof or release doc, and required to appear in either release language or sales caveat copy before being discussed externally.
- This register is the single source of truth for May 1. New caveats land here with their proof link; release language and sales caveat copy then carry the gist.
